Things that I enjoy doing...

Sports

Sports are a blast. Whether it's listening to sports radio and podcasts, or playing with my 8 year old daughter, it always been a large part of my life. Watching her learn and grow in her own way is the best part. Sports, unless professionally, aren't tyically about the winning; it's about the experience and learning important life lessons along the way.

Traveling

Traveling, learning new languages, and understanding different cultures has always interested me. After 7 years of Spanish, visiting other countries, and my stint in the melting pot that is the United States Army, that interest has written itself in my DNA. If someone never ventures out of their own comfort zone culturally, they miss out on the people they could meet, and never fully understand the world we live in outside of the myopic walls.

Learning

Having a thirst for knowledge has guided me throughout my life. My daughter now has developed a similar way of being. We spend our time reading, questioning, and never settling for letting information come to us. We search for it in an attempt to build out our mental storage units. If only I was as smart as she will be!
